## Dashboard Description

This Grafana dashboard visualizes **EV charging session data** from *BigQuery*.  
The **top left panel** highlights the total number of charging sessions (**900**), providing an at-a-glance view of total activity.  
The **line chart** on the right tracks *daily EV charging sessions over time*, showing an increase in usage beginning around mid-year.  
The **bar chart** below compares *average kWh by membership tier*, revealing that **members** tend to consume slightly more energy per session than **fleet users**.  

**Key Insight:** EV charging demand spikes mid-year, suggesting higher travel or usage patterns during that period.
